Hayden Grech Photography logo

The logo was created for a photographer who wanted a fresh design for his watermark to place on his photo.

The process began with researching existing logos to understand the direction we needed to take. After combining my research with Hayden's insights, I moved on to exploring fonts on 1001 Fonts.

Next, I started designing in Adobe Illustrator. Initially, the text alone felt a bit flat, so I decided to incorporate a simple illustration of a Nikon Z6 II. This addition brought more character and depth to the logo.

I'm really grateful to Hayden Grech for trusting me with this project and allowing me to bring his vision to life.
