Ghost with a Pumpkin

Roll out the hot chocolates with cookies and put away your swimwear! September has finally arrived.

Autumn is my favourite season. The warm colour schemes and the cosy nights with your hot chocolate in hand while covering yourself with a blanket to keep warm from the changing weather is something I look forward to as soon summer starts.

Here's a simple drawing of a ghost carrying a pumpkin. Who's ready for a pumpkin spice latte?

This drawing is another drawing that could be tattooed on a person, I guess I found my style for the upcoming months.
