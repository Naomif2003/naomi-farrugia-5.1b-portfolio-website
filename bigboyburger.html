Big Boy Burger logo

One of the tasks assigned this semester was to design a logo for a fictional burger joint called "Big Boy Burger."

Before finalizing the design, the first step was to gather information about the restaurant. This included understanding its location, the type of food being served, and the overall environment. In this case, the burger joint would operate out of a food truck located in Bahar ic Caghaq.

The next step involved researching logos from similar businesses to ensure the right colour scheme and font choices. Sketching played a key role in the process, as it allowed me to explore different design concepts and find the most fitting direction.

The final logo was created using Adobe Illustrator.
