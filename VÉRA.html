VÉRA logo & poster

I asked ChatGPT to give me a random concept for a brand, this is what I got.

Brand Name: VÉRA
Industry: Skincare & Wellness
Launch Date: Fall 2025
Brand Slogan: "Rooted in nature. Raised by ritual."

Overview: VÉRA is a premium, plant-powered skincare and wellness brand designed for the conscious consumer. Born out of a desire to reconnect people with clean, effective self-care rituals, VÉRA combines botanical science with modern sustainability. The brand's philosophy is simple: skincare should be honest, holistic, and healing — from the inside out.

Brand Personality: Modern: Sleek, intuitive design that reflects clarity and purpose Grounded: Rooted in nature, with authentic storytelling and traceable ingredients Empowering: Encourages intentional self-care and self-respect.
