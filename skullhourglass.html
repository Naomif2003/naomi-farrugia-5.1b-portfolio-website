Hourglass, Rose, Skull

The typical rose and skull that plenty of individuals get tattooed on them. A lion and a clock would have completed this look.

While drawing this, I was going for something... simpler. During the drawing process, I kept on getting newer ideas. In the end, I decided to add those visions to create a more 'bigger, art piece.

Now, that's finished, I'm happy about it. It's something I've never done before. As an artist, I feel like drawing skulls is the basic piece of drawing I should know.
