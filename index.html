About me

Hi, I’m Naomi Farrugia from Malta, EU. I’m a junior graphic designer currently pursuing my bachelor's degree at MCAST. In this portfolio, you’ll find a selection of my best work, including digital design, digital illustrations, and photography. I consider myself a creative individual, always eager to develop and expand my skills. I'm excited for the opportunities to grow and refine my craft.

Currently, I hold; an MQF level 3 in Multimedia and ICT Applications (Alternative Learning Programme, ALP), a Diploma in Art and Design degree, an Advanced Diploma in Digital Design and working on my bachelor's Digital Design. (MCAST).
