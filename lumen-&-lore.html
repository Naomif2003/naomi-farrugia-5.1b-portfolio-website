Lumen & Lore logo & poster

I asked ChatGPT to give me a random concept for a brand, this is what I got.

Brand Name: Lumen & Lore
Launch Date: September 2025
Category: Luxury Lifestyle & Home Fragrance
Tagline: "Illuminate Your Story."
Mindful Luxury: Luxurious without excess; elegant, not ostentatious.

Overview: Lumen & Lore is a premium lifestyle brand set to redefine the way we experience space, memory, and mood through scent and design. Drawing inspiration from ancient storytelling traditions and modern minimalist aesthetics, the brand offers hand-crafted candles, incense, room sprays, and ambient lighting designed to evoke emotion and atmosphere.

Founders: Elena Moreau, a former interior designer with a background in aromatherapy, and Malik Chen, a creative technologist focused on sustainable materials and sensory experience.

Visual Identity: A muted palette of earthy tones, soft blacks, and antique golds. Typography blends modern serif fonts with handwritten elements. The logo features a minimalist flame motif encased in a storybook emblem.
