Titan's POP! Vinyl Collection logo

Here's an update to a logo I originally created for TITAN'S POP! Vinyl Collection about a year ago.

The main reason for this redesign was that the previous logo was made in Adobe Photoshop, which resulted in a raster image rather than a vector. This change allowed for better scalability and versatility.

The second and third logos are alternative styles I designed, giving the client a variety of options to choose from.

A huge thank you to Ethan Mallia for trusting me with this opportunity to improve and refine my skills.
