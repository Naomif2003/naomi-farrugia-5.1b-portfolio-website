Skull

I drew this to experience how the facial structure is built from the inside. I find this practice useful as skulls are usually drawn similarly to each other. Skulls are the 'basic' drawings that people draw, especially tattoo artists. As a person that loves tattoos, I find myself practising objects that are more commonly drawn by my tattoo artist.

Overall, every drawing can be better but as of now, I'm happy with the final artwork. Just a few weeks ago, I wouldn't have thought I could create something like this.
